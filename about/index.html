About SavingsClub

Helping Americans make smarter money decisions with free tools and expert guidance.

Our Mission

SavingsClub was founded on a simple belief: every American deserves access to high-quality financial tools and education — regardless of income, background, or financial literacy level. We provide free, unbiased financial calculators and expert-written guides that help everyday people make confident money decisions.

Too many financial tools are locked behind paywalls, require account signups, or are designed to push specific products. SavingsClub is different. Our calculators are 100% free, require no registration, and process your financial data entirely within your browser — we never see your numbers.

What We Offer

17 Free Financial Calculators

From savings and budgeting to mortgages, retirement, and debt payoff — our calculators cover the full spectrum of personal finance decisions. Each calculator includes visual charts, detailed breakdowns, and actionable next steps. Every tool is designed to be simple enough for beginners yet comprehensive enough for experienced planners.

Expert Financial Guides

Our editorial team publishes in-depth personal finance articles three times per week. We cover topics including saving strategies, credit card comparisons, banking options, debt management, retirement planning, tax strategies, and state-specific financial guidance for all 50 states.

Unbiased Product Comparisons

We research and compare banking products, credit cards, and financial tools from FDIC-insured institutions across the country. Our comparisons are based on objective criteria: interest rates, fees, features, and user experience. While we may earn affiliate commissions from some products, our editorial recommendations are never influenced by these partnerships.

Our Editorial Standards

Transparency and accuracy are at the core of everything we publish. Our editorial standards include:

  • Independence: Affiliate relationships never influence our recommendations or editorial content
  • Accuracy: All financial data, rates, and product information are verified and updated regularly
  • Clarity: Complex financial concepts are explained in plain, accessible language
  • Transparency: Affiliate relationships and potential conflicts of interest are clearly disclosed
  • Timeliness: Content is reviewed and updated to reflect current rates, regulations, and market conditions

Privacy First

Your financial privacy matters to us. The numbers you enter into our calculators are processed locally in your web browser and are never transmitted to our servers. We don't require account registration, email addresses, or personal information to use any of our tools. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Who We Serve

SavingsClub is designed for all Americans — from recent graduates just starting their financial journey to families managing complex budgets to retirees optimizing their savings. Our content addresses the financial needs and challenges specific to residents of all 50 states, accounting for differences in state taxes, cost of living, and local regulations.

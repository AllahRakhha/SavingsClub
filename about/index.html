About SavingsClub

We believe every American deserves free access to the same financial tools and knowledge that the wealthy take for granted.

🎯 Our Mission

SavingsClub exists to democratize financial literacy. We build free, high-quality calculators and publish honest personal finance guides for Americans across all 50 states. No paywalls, no required signups, no hidden agendas. We believe that understanding your money shouldn't cost money.

🔓 Your Privacy, Our Promise

Every calculator on SavingsClub runs 100% in your browser. Your financial data never leaves your device — we never see it, store it, or sell it. Unlike many financial sites, we don't require you to create an account or hand over your email just to use a savings calculator. Your numbers are yours alone.

What We Offer

17 Free Financial Calculators — From savings and compound interest to mortgages, 401(k)s, Roth IRAs, and take-home pay. Each calculator includes visual charts, detailed breakdowns, and educational content to help you understand your results.

Expert Personal Finance Guides — Our editorial team publishes in-depth articles three times a week covering savings strategies, credit card optimization, debt payoff methods, retirement planning, and banking. Every guide is written for real American households — not Wall Street traders.

Credit Card Comparisons — Side-by-side comparisons of top credit cards including cash back, travel rewards, balance transfer, and cards for building credit. We highlight the features that matter: APR, annual fees, reward rates, and sign-up bonuses.

Banking Resources — Curated lists of the best high-yield savings accounts, free checking accounts, and current bank bonuses available to Americans nationwide. We only feature FDIC-insured institutions.

💰 How We Keep SavingsClub Free

SavingsClub is completely free for every user. To cover operating costs and continue developing new tools, we may receive referral fees when visitors choose to explore financial products through our partner links. These partnerships allow us to maintain and improve our calculators, publish expert guides, and keep everything accessible at no cost to you. Importantly, our tools function identically regardless of any business relationships, and our editorial recommendations are based solely on what we believe serves our readers best. For complete details, please see our Affiliate Disclosure.

📝 Editorial Independence

Our editorial content — blog posts, calculator explanations, and educational guides — is written independently from our business relationships. No advertiser or affiliate partner reviews our content before publication. If a product is bad, we say so. If a strategy doesn't work for most people, we explain why. Our readers' financial wellbeing comes first, every time.

🇺🇸 Built for All 50 States

Whether you're in New York City or rural Montana, coastal California or suburban Ohio, SavingsClub's tools and guides are designed for your financial reality. We reference state-specific details where they matter — cost of living differences, state tax implications, regional banking options, and local assistance programs. Personal finance isn't one-size-fits-all, and neither is SavingsClub.

🔒 Security & Trust

SavingsClub is built on enterprise-grade infrastructure with industry-leading security standards. We implement Content Security Policy headers, X-Frame-Options protection, strict referrer policies, and full HTTPS encryption across every page. Our architecture is designed with security-first principles — there are no databases to breach, no stored user credentials, and no server-side vulnerabilities. All financial data you enter into our calculators remains entirely within your browser and is never transmitted to our servers. We exclusively feature FDIC-insured financial institutions in our comparisons and recommendations.

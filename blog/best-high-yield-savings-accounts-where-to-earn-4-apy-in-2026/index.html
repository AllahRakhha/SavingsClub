Best High-Yield Savings Accounts: Where to Earn 4%+ APY in 2026

By SavingsClub Research Team · January 13, 2026

Rates as of early 2026. Verify current rates with providers.

Why High-Yield Savings Matters

The national average savings rate remains below 0.50% APY, while top online banks offer 4.00-4.50% APY. On $10,000, that difference is $400+ per year in free money. Every American, whether in Texas, Florida, or Ohio, should be earning competitive rates on their savings.

Top Accounts

Marcus by Goldman Sachs, Ally Bank, Discover Online Savings, and Capital One 360 Performance consistently lead with no minimums, no fees, and FDIC insurance up to $250,000. All are available nationwide.

What to Look For

Compare APY (not just introductory rates), minimum balance requirements, fee structures, transfer speeds, and mobile app quality. Some banks offer rate bumps for larger balances. Use our Savings Calculator to see how much your savings could grow at different rates.

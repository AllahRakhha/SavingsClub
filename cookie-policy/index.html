Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website operators information about how their site is being used.

2. How SavingsClub Uses Cookies

We use the following categories of cookies:

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as remembering your cookie consent preference, maintaining session state (e.g., admin panel login), and storing your calculator preferences locally in your browser. Essential cookies cannot be disabled without affecting site functionality. These cookies do not collect personal information and are set by SavingsClub (first-party cookies).

2.2 Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our content, tools, and user experience. Analytics cookies may collect information about pages visited and time spent on each page, links clicked, browser and device information, approximate geographic location (city/state level), and referral source (how you arrived at our site). We may use third-party analytics services such as Google Analytics, which sets its own cookies. Google Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.

2.3 Advertising and Affiliate Cookies

When you click on affiliate links or view advertisements on our site, our advertising partners and affiliate networks may set cookies on your device. These cookies are used to track referrals so we receive credit for purchases made through our links, serve relevant advertisements based on your interests, measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns, and prevent showing you the same ad repeatedly. Third-party advertising cookies are set by our partners, not by SavingsClub. Each partner has its own privacy and cookie policy.

3. Cookie Duration

Session cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period (ranging from 30 days to 2 years) or until you delete them manually.

4. Managing Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings menu. Note that blocking all cookies may affect site functionality.

Our cookie banner: When you first visit our site, you can accept or decline non-essential cookies through our cookie consent banner.

Third-party opt-outs: You can opt out of interest-based advertising through the Network Advertising Initiative, the Digital Advertising Alliance, or individual advertising partner settings.

5. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage to save your cookie consent preference, store admin panel session data, and save any blog posts created through the admin panel. Local storage data remains on your device until you clear your browser data. You can clear local storage through your browser's developer tools or settings.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. For significant changes, we will display a new cookie consent banner.

7. Contact

Questions about our cookie practices? Contact us at privacy@savingsclub.com.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and supply information to website owners. This Cookie Policy explains how SavingsClub ("we," "us," "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on savingsclub.com ("Website").

How We Use Cookies

We use the following types of cookies on our Website:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Website to function properly. They enable core features such as remembering your cookie consent preferences. Without these cookies, certain features of the Website may not be available.

  • sc_cookies — Stores your cookie consent preference (accept/decline). Duration: 1 year.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our Website. This helps us improve our content, layout, and overall user experience. These cookies collect information in an aggregated, anonymized form.

  • Google Analytics (_ga, _ga_*, _gid) — Tracks page views, session duration, traffic sources, and user behavior. Duration: up to 2 years. Provider: Google LLC.

Affiliate Tracking Cookies

When you click on affiliate links on our Website, our affiliate partners may place cookies on your device to track the referral. These cookies help our partners attribute any subsequent qualifying actions (such as account openings) to our Website.

  • CJ Affiliate cookies — Track referrals to financial product partners. Duration: varies (typically 30-90 days). Provider: CJ Affiliate (Conversant LLC).
  • Impact.com cookies — Track referrals to financial product partners. Duration: varies (typically 30-90 days). Provider: Impact Tech, Inc.

Your Cookie Choices

When you first visit our Website, a cookie consent banner allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by:

  • Clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the Website (the consent banner will reappear)
  • Adjusting your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Using browser extensions that manage cookie consent

Browser Cookie Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically find these options in your browser's "Privacy" or "Security" settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features on our Website. Links to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Safari
  • Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge

Do Not Track Signals

Some web browsers may transmit "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals. Our Website currently does not respond to DNT signals. However, you can manage your cookie preferences through the methods described above.

Third-Party Cookies

Please note that third parties (such as Google Analytics and affiliate networks) set their own cookies, which are governed by their respective privacy and cookie policies. We do not control these third-party cookies.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Changes will be indicated by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at privacy@savingsclub.com or visit our Contact page.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about SavingsClub.

Are these calculators really free?
Yes — completely free. No signup, no email required, no premium tier. All 17 calculators on SavingsClub are 100% free to use as many times as you need.
Is my financial data saved or shared?
No. Everything you enter stays in your browser. We don't store, save, or transmit your financial data anywhere. Close the tab and the numbers are gone.
How accurate are these calculators?
Our calculators use standard financial formulas. Results are estimates intended for educational planning. For major financial decisions, always consult a licensed financial advisor.
Why don't you require a signup?
Because financial tools should be accessible to everyone. We make money through affiliate partnerships, not by collecting or selling your data.
Who creates the content and calculators?
SavingsClub Research Team — humans using AI tools to research and produce educational content, with review before publication. We are not licensed financial advisors and we disclose this clearly on every page.
How often is the content updated?
We publish new blog content three times per week — Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. Calculators are reviewed regularly to ensure formulas and default rates stay current.

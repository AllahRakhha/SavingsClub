How to Start Investing

You don't need thousands to begin. Fractional shares and zero-commission brokerages mean anyone can start with $100. Buy a total market index fund for instant diversification across hundreds of companies. Set up automatic weekly contributions and let compound growth do the work.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good rate of return?

The S&P 500 has historically returned about 10% annually before inflation, or roughly 7% after inflation. Use 7-8% for long-term projections as a reasonable estimate.

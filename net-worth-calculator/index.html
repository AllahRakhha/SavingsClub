Why Track Net Worth?

Your net worth — assets minus liabilities — is the single best measure of financial progress. Income tells you how much flows in; net worth tells you how much you've kept. Tracking it monthly or quarterly gives you a clear picture of whether you're moving forward.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average American net worth?

The median net worth for American households is approximately $192,900 according to the Federal Reserve. However, this varies significantly by age, with households headed by someone 55-64 having median net worth around $364,000.
